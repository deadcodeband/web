bio

The hard-rock/alt-metal quintet Deadcode feature; Matt McGuire (vocals), Eric Happe (guitars), Josh Brewer (guitars/keys), Brandon Happe (bass), and Davis Brown (drums). Prior to Deadcode, Eric and Brandon were members of the eclectic instrumental act Protoplanet, while Davis was a founding member of the blues-rock Ty Curtis band, and Josh is a member of the progressive-metal band Duskâ€™s Embrace. Deadcode formed in 2015 in Portland, and together they create a complex clamor of intense vocals, sprawling guitars, propulsive drumming, and heavy basslines. This year, the group will release their debut album and accompany that with multi-city performances.
